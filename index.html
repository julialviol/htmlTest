Iron Man received numerous awards and nominations. Some of which are the most prestigious awards in the film industry such as Saturn Awards and the Satellite Awards. The list includes:

Saturn Awards:

  • Best Actor
  • Best Science Fiction Film
  • 2x Best Director

Central Ohio Film Critics:

  • Actor of the Year

Satellite Awards:

  • Best Film Editing
  • Best DVD Extra

MTV Movie Awards:

  • Best Summer Movie You Haven't Seen Yet
